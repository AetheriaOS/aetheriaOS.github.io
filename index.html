What is AetheriaOS?

AetheriaOS is a custom ROM built to stay simple on the surface while still giving you the tools power users look for. No clutter, no unnecessary friction — just a smooth, reliable experience with a thoughtful set of extra features layered on top, wrapped in a clean and modern look.

Base: LineageOS
